What Is CurbPHX?





CurbPHX is a comprehensive digital inventory of all sidewalks and curb ramps in the city. This data bank can record features such as width, obstruction, curb height, or other accessibility issues. Simply, CurbPHX utilizes image recognition to identify sidewalks all over phoenix.
